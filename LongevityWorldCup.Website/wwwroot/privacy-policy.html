Privacy policy

How Longevity World Cup handles website operations and social publishing integrations.

Who we are

Longevity World Cup operates the website at longevityworldcup.com and related social publishing workflows, including Threads and Facebook.

What data is used

For the Threads integration, the application uses the Threads app ID, Threads app secret, and Threads access token configured by the operator. For the Facebook integration, the application uses the Facebook app ID, Facebook app secret, Facebook page ID, and Facebook page access token configured by the operator. These credentials are used only to authenticate with the relevant Meta APIs and publish content on behalf of the connected Threads profile or Facebook Page.

How the data is used

The Threads and Facebook integrations are used to:

  • authenticate with the relevant Meta APIs,
  • publish Longevity World Cup updates to the connected Threads profile and Facebook Page,
  • refresh access tokens when required for continued operation.

What data is not used

The Threads and Facebook integrations are not intended as general user login systems. They are not designed to collect data from unrelated users or to process private social content beyond what is required for the connected Threads profile or Facebook Page to publish posts.

Storage and retention

Configured API credentials may be stored by the Longevity World Cup operator in local or server configuration files required for the website to function. They are retained only for as long as needed to operate and maintain the integration.

Third-party services

The Threads and Facebook integrations depend on Meta and the relevant Meta APIs. Use of Threads and Facebook is also subject to Meta's own terms and privacy practices.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or want to request deletion of data directly controlled by Longevity World Cup, contact: hi@longevityworldcup.com.
