How Bad Do You Want It?

To calculate your biological age, get a blood test — but first, choose your track:

  • White Blood Cell Count (WBC)
  • Lymphocytes
  • Mean Corpuscular Volume (MCV)
  • Red Cell Distribution Width (RDW)
  • Albumin
  • Alkaline Phosphatase (ALP)
  • Creatinine
  • Glucose (Blood Sugar)
  • C-Reactive Protein (CRP)

Visit any lab for these tests — just pay the lab fee. It might even be covered by your healthcare provider.
